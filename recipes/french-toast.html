Simple French Toast

Description

This quick and easy french toast recipe is prefect for all occasions from early mornings to a late night snack! Make this a staple in your household!

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of flour (all purpose)
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 8 slices of bread

Directions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.

  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour and eggs followed by the rest of the ingredients.

  3. Dip your bread slices into your batter, making sure to coat them well. Then, carefully place them on your skillet.

  4. Cook and flip until both sides of the bread are golden brown. Serve warm, optionally with syrup and fresh fruit. Enjoy!
