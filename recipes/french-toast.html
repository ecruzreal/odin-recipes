Simple French Toast

Description

This quick and easy french toast recipe is prefect for all occasions from early mornings to a late night snack! Make this a staple in your household!

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of flour (all purpose)
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 8 slices of bread
