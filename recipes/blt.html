BLT Sandwich

Description

This simple yet delicous BLT Sandwich is perfect for a light lunch meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 2 (or more!) strips of thin cut bacon
  • 1-2 pieces of romaine lettuce
  • 3-4 slices of your favorite tomato (I use roma tomatoes)
  • 2 teaspoons of mayo
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Once up to temperature, add your strips of bacon; cook to desired doneness. (I reccommend crsipy!)

  2. Remove the cooked bacon and let sit on a paper towel to absorb excess oil.

  3. On your slices of bread, evenly spread the mayonaise and add your lettuce and tomato.

  4. This part is really important: Season your tomatoes with salt and pepper! (Sometimes I use lemon pepper seasoning and it is delicious) I cannot stress enough how important it is to season your tomatoes, people!

  5. Add your bacon and close up your sandwich. Optionally toast your bread for a nice crunch, enjoy!
