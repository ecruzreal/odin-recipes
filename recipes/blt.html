BLT Sandwich

Description

This simple yet delicous BLT Sandwich is perfect for a light lunch meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 2 (or more!) strips of thin cut bacon
  • 1-2 pieces of romaine lettuce
  • 3-4 slices of your favorite tomato (I use roma tomatoes)
  • 2 teaspoons of mayo
  • salt and pepper to taste
