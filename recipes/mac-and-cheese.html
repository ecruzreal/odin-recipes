Kickin' Mac and Cheese

Description

This simple mac and cheese recipe with a spciy kick is perfect for busy nights when you're craving a delicously creamy bowl of mac and cheese! Using simple ingredients, you can have this homemade alternative to the boxed stuff ready in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of elbow macaroni (8 ounces)
  • 1 tablespoon of neutral oil
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of flour (all purpose)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • black pepper to your liking
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons of buffalo sauce
  • 1/4 cup diced jalapeño
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of your favorite melting cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add your pasta along with the oil and a pinch of salt; cook until al dente.

  2. Melt butter in a medium sized sauce pan over medium heat.

  3. Add your flour, salt, garlic powder, black pepper, and paprika to make your roux and stir until smooth

  4. Pour in your milk one cup at a time, slowly while stirring. Continue mixing until smooth and simmering.

  5. Add your cheese, jalapeños, and buffalo sauce and stir until the cheese has melted.

  6. Fold in your cooked pasta until thoroughly coated. Optionally garnish with jalapeños and serve warm, enjoy!
